# Navigate Viewport

The Viewport is the panel where you view and edit your level.

## Look around

1. Hold the **right mouse button**.
2. Move the mouse.

The camera rotates in the direction you move the mouse.

---

## Move the camera

1. Hold the **right mouse button**.
2. Press the following keys:

| Key | Action        |
| --- | ------------- |
| W   | Move forward  |
| S   | Move backward |
| A   | Move left     |
| D   | Move right    |
| Q   | Move down     |
| E   | Move up       |

---

## Move faster or slower

Hold the **right mouse button**, then use:

* **Mouse wheel up** — Move faster
* **Mouse wheel down** — Move slower

---

## Move while holding middle mouse button (optional)

Hold the **middle mouse button**, then move the mouse.

The camera pans left, right, up, or down.
