# Convert CRW to PNG

1. Read the message.
    ![Screenshot](images/convert-crw-to-png01.png) 

1. Select **Install**.
    ![Screenshot](images/convert-crw-to-png02.png) 

1. Click the download link.
    ![Screenshot](images/convert-crw-to-png03.png) 

1. Download the installer.
    ![Screenshot](images/convert-crw-to-png04.png)

1. Click **More info**.
    ![Screenshot](images/convert-crw-to-png05.png)

1. Click **Next**.
    ![Screenshot](images/convert-crw-to-png06.png)

1. Click **Next**.
    ![Screenshot](images/convert-crw-to-png07.png)

1. Click **Next**.
    ![Screenshot](images/convert-crw-to-png08.png)

1. Click **Finish**.
    ![Screenshot](images/convert-crw-to-png09.png)

1. Open the **CRW** file.
    ![Screenshot](images/convert-crw-to-png10.png)

1. Select **Export**.
    ![Screenshot](images/convert-crw-to-png11.png)

1. Adjust the settings, then export.
    ![Screenshot](images/convert-crw-to-png12.png)

1. The conversion is complete.
    ![Screenshot](images/convert-crw-to-png13.png)
